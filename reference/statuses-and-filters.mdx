This page defines the filters users see most often in Grievly.

## Statuses

### Open

Open grievances are active cases that have not been resolved.

### Resolved

Resolved grievances have a completed resolution record.

Resolved cases are read-only in most areas. Admins can reopen them if new information arrives.

### Pending approval

Pending approval means the grievance has been submitted for resolution approval and required signers have not all signed yet.

Pending approval is not a saved grievance status. The grievance remains open and unresolved until the approval workflow completes.

## Derived queues

Derived queues are calculated from grievance data. They are not separate saved statuses.

### Overdue

An open grievance is overdue when its due date is in the past.

Resolved grievances do not appear as overdue.

### High Priority

An open grievance is High Priority when it has been marked for faster operator attention.

Resolved High Priority grievances no longer appear in the High Priority queue.

### My queue

My queue shows grievances assigned to you.

It also shows grievances waiting for your resolution approval when you are a required signer.

## Common list filters

The grievance list can filter by:

- Facility.
- Status.
- Category.
- F-tag.
- Assignee.
- Received date range.
- Search text.
- Resident Council submission.
- Employee involved.
- Third party concern.
- Anonymous reporter.

The Status filter includes **Pending approval** for open grievances with an active resolution approval request.

## Search

Search can match visible grievance details such as grievance ID, title, resident information, category, concern text, and other searchable case fields.

If search results look too narrow, clear filters first and then search again.

## Sorting

The grievance list can sort by common fields such as created date, received date, SLA, and grievance ID.

Sorting changes the order of results. It does not change which records match the current filters.

## Access scope

Filters only operate inside the data you can access.

Admins filter across the organization. Members filter within assigned facilities.

---
title: "Share public intake links"
description: "Let residents, families, and representatives submit concerns through a facility intake link."
---

Public intake links let people submit concerns without signing in to Grievly.

Users with access to a facility can view its public intake URL and download its QR assets.

## Find a facility intake link

1. Open **Facilities**.
2. Choose the facility.
3. Open the **Details** tab.
4. Find the **Public intake** panel.
5. Copy the link.

Each active facility has a permanent public intake URL and QR code.

Public intake is unavailable while a facility is inactive.

## Download a QR poster

From the facility's public intake panel, select **Download poster** for a printable poster or **Download PNG** for the QR image by itself.

Use the poster in approved facility channels where residents, families, representatives, or staff should be able to submit a concern.

## Share the link

You can share the link with approved facility channels such as:

- Resident or family handouts
- Internal facility materials
- A QR code
- A facility website page
- Admission or orientation packets

## Review submitted concerns

Submissions create records for facility review. Review the intake details, confirm routing, and assign follow-up before working the case.

## Good intake link placement

Use clear language around the link, such as "Submit a concern" or "Share a grievance or complaint." This helps residents and families recognize that concerns, complaints, and grievances can all be submitted through the same channel.

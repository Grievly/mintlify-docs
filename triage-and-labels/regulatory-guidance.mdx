CareResolve connects an F-tagged grievance to federal nursing-home guidance from CMS Appendix PP.

<Warning>
  This feature is an operational reference, not legal advice. It covers federal Appendix PP only and does not determine compliance or account for state-specific reporting, deadlines, forms, or requirements.
</Warning>

## Suggested next steps

The first time an open F-tagged grievance is viewed, CareResolve can prepare a collapsed **Suggested next steps** list from the case and selected tag.

<Frame caption="Suggested next steps stay inside the AI summary and remain advisory.">
  <img src="/images/regulatory-guidance.webp" alt="Expanded suggested next steps for an F-tagged grievance" />
</Frame>

Expanding the list does not update fields, assign work, notify anyone, or change the case status. Changing the F-tag prepares a new set against the replacement tag.

## Open the guidance

Select an F-tag badge or **View guidance**. The guidance window includes:

- **Guidance:** plain-language summary, survey focus, common failure modes, and high-risk triggers.
- **How to prepare:** quick checks, immediate fixes, records to audit, observations, interview prompts, and evidence to have ready.
- **Source:** stored Appendix PP source text and a link to the CMS nursing-home guidance page.

The first two tabs are AI-generated from the federal source. Verify important language against **Source** and current CMS material.

## Compliance review after resolution

The first time a resolved F-tagged grievance is viewed, CareResolve compares its documented resolution with the selected tag's guidance. The collapsed **Compliance review** shows **Aligned**, **Minor gaps**, or **Significant gaps** and can be expanded for its assessment.

The review is saved as a snapshot. It does not reopen the grievance, change the resolution, make a reporting decision, or determine compliance. Reopen through the normal workflow when staff decide more work is required.

## Availability by case state

| Case state | Guidance | Suggested next steps | Compliance review |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Open with an F-tag | Available | Generated automatically | Not shown |
| Pending approval with an F-tag | Available | Not shown | Not shown |
| Resolved with an F-tag | Available | Not shown | Generated automatically |
| No F-tag | Not available | Not shown | Not shown |

Assistant can also answer questions tied to an F-tag or search Appendix PP across several tags. Citations open the same guidance window.

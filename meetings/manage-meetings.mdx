title: "Manage meeting notes"
description: "Create, search, edit, lock, and review facility meeting records in CareResolve."
---

Meetings is a facility-scoped workspace for dated notes and grievance follow-up. Use it for Resident Council meetings and other facility meetings that need a searchable, attributable record.

## Create a meeting

1. Open **Meetings** from the sidebar.
2. Select **New meeting**.
3. Enter a title, confirm the facility, choose the date, and optionally add a start time.
4. Select **Create**.

CareResolve opens the full meeting page so you can begin taking notes. Members can create meetings in facilities they can access; administrators can create them in any active facility in the organization.

## Take notes

The meeting title, date, time, and notes save as you work. The activity control shows save state and who created, last edited, or locked the meeting.

Type `/` at the start of a block to add:

- A grievance reference.
- Text or a heading.
- A bulleted, numbered, or to-do list.
- A quote or divider.

Select text to apply inline formatting. Use to-do lists for follow-up items that belong in the meeting record.

### Reference a grievance in the notes

Type `#`, then search for an open or resolved grievance from the same facility. Select the grievance to insert a navigable reference card into the notes.

This note reference provides context only. It is different from recording the meeting as the grievance's Resident Council source. Use the grievance intake record for that provenance link.

## Find and open meetings

The Meetings page groups records by date, including Upcoming, Today, recent periods, and older months.

- Use the facility selector to narrow the list.
- Use meeting search to match titles and note text.
- Select a row to open its preview sheet.
- Use the expand action when you want the full meeting page.

Search and facility results always stay inside your facility access.

## Linked grievances

When a grievance records this meeting as its Resident Council source, the meeting displays a **Linked grievances** section above the notes. Each row shows the grievance ID, title, and open or resolved state and links to the case.

The meeting page is read-only for this relationship. To change or remove the source link, edit the grievance's internal intake record.

## Autosave and concurrent edits

CareResolve protects meeting notes from silently overwriting a newer edit. If the meeting changed elsewhere, use **View latest** or the activity refresh action before editing again.

If a save indicator remains in an error state, keep the page open and refresh to the latest version before re-entering any unsaved change.

## Lock or delete a meeting

Only administrators can use the meeting action menu to lock, unlock, or delete a meeting.

- **Lock:** makes the title, date, time, and notes read-only for everyone until an administrator unlocks the meeting.
- **Unlock:** returns the meeting to normal editing.
- **Delete:** permanently removes the meeting and its notes.

CareResolve blocks deletion while any grievances use the meeting as their source. Remove those links from the grievance intake records first.

<Warning>
  Deleting a meeting cannot be undone. Lock a completed meeting when the goal is to preserve it as read-only evidence.
</Warning>

These docs are written for administrators, compliance leaders, facility staff, and team members who need to keep the grievance log accurate and current.

Most pages explain the daily workflow first. Admin-only setup and workspace controls are grouped separately so members can focus on the work they can do.

## Daily workflow

1. Review the dashboard and queue counts.
2. Capture new grievances, complaints, and concerns as soon as they are received.
3. Assign ownership and routing details.
4. Work each case from the grievance timeline.
5. Use comments, mentions, subscriptions, and attachments to keep the record current.
6. Resolve the grievance only after the outcome and required communication are documented.
7. Use patterns, analytics, reports, and Assistant for review, not as replacements for human judgment.

## What belongs in Grievly

Use Grievly for resident grievances, complaints, concerns, suggestions that require follow-up, family or representative reports, public intake submissions, and documentation tied to grievance resolution.

Avoid adding unnecessary sensitive information. Only document the details the facility needs to review, follow up on, and support the resolution.

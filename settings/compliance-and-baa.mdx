---

The Business Associate Agreement must be active before a workspace processes resident PHI.

Only administrators can complete or review workspace compliance setup.

## During first setup

If the agreement is not active, administrators see the setup step needed to complete it.

Members see a waiting screen asking them to contact an administrator.

## Compliance settings

Use **Settings > Compliance** to review agreement status and related compliance documents.

After the BAA is signed, the page shows the signer and signed date. Administrators can also download the signed agreement as a PDF for their records.

## PHI reminder

Grievly is designed for grievance operations, but users still need to document carefully.

Do not put resident names, room numbers, narrative text, audio, attachments, or other PHI into outside tools, analytics, support messages, or systems that are not approved for that information.

For more detail, see [Privacy and PHI basics](/reference/privacy-and-phi).

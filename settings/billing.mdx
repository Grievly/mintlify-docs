---
title: "Billing and activation"
description: "Understand workspace billing readiness and how facility activation is handled."
---

Billing readiness is one of the setup gates that must be complete before a workspace can be used.

Grievly manages billing and facility activation with your account team. There is no self-service Billing section in product Settings.

## During first setup

After an administrator signs the Business Associate Agreement, the workspace may show **Complete billing to continue** until Grievly activates billing.

All users remain on this readiness screen until activation is complete.

## Facility activation

Grievly provisions and activates each facility for the organization. Customers do not create, archive, or restore facility profiles from the product app.

After your account manager confirms activation, refresh the page or use **Check again** on the facility setup screen.

## Get help

Contact your Grievly account manager to:

- Complete or confirm billing activation.
- Add another facility.
- Update a facility profile.
- Change a facility's activation status.

If you cannot reach your account manager, contact [support@grievly.ai](mailto:support@grievly.ai).

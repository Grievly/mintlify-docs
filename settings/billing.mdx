---
title: "Billing"
description: "Manage payment setup, plan status, and facility capacity."
---

Billing controls whether the workspace is ready for daily use and how many facilities the organization can operate in Grievly.

Only administrators can manage billing.

## First payment setup

After the Business Associate Agreement is active, an administrator completes payment setup.

Members see a waiting screen until this step is complete.

## Billing settings

Open **Settings > Billing** to review plan, payment setup, billing status, and facility capacity.

## Facility capacity

Facility capacity controls how many active facilities the workspace can use.

If an administrator cannot add another facility, check billing and capacity first.

## Troubleshooting

If billing appears incomplete after setup, refresh the page. If the issue remains, contact support at [support@grievly.ai](mailto:support@grievly.ai).

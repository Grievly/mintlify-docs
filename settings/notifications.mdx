Notification settings control which new-grievance and overdue events are delivered to you and whether assignment notifications also send email. Grievance subscriptions and case participation continue to drive other activity notifications.

## Manage your preferences

Open **Settings > Notifications**. You can choose:

- **New grievances**: select the facilities where you want a notification when a grievance is created.
- **Assignment emails**: choose whether an assignment notification also sends email. The in-app assignment notification still appears when email is off.
- **Overdue grievances**: receive notifications about overdue grievances in facilities you can access, even when you are not the assignee.

Only active facilities in your access scope are available. Select **Save** after making changes. **Cancel** restores the last saved choices.

## Manage a teammate's preferences

Administrators can manage notification preferences for another user:

1. Open **Team**.
2. Select the teammate.
3. Open the **Notifications** tab.
4. Choose facilities and email types.
5. Select **Save**.

For a member, the facility choices are limited to that person's assigned facilities. An administrator can receive notifications for any active facility.

## Grievance subscriptions

Notification settings are separate from subscribing to an individual grievance. A subscription sends activity updates for that case, and Grievly may subscribe you automatically when you create or interact with it.

Notification preferences control which new-grievance, assignment, and overdue updates Grievly sends to you by email. Grievance subscriptions and case participation continue to drive other in-app activity notifications.

## Manage your preferences

Select your avatar in the upper-right corner, choose **Account settings**, and open **Notifications**. You can also open the notification bell, use its three-dot action menu, and select **Manage notifications**.

Choose:

- **New grievances**: select the facilities where you want email when a grievance is created. Search the facility selector or use **Select all** and **Deselect all**.
- **Assignment emails**: choose whether an assignment also sends email. The in-app assignment notification still appears when email is off.
- **Overdue grievances**: receive email about overdue grievances in facilities you can access, even when you are not the assignee.

Only active facilities in your access scope are available. **Save** and **Cancel** appear after you change a preference. **Cancel** restores the last saved choices.

## Manage a teammate's preferences

Administrators can manage notification preferences for another user:

1. Open **Team**.
2. Select the teammate.
3. Open the **Notifications** tab.
4. Choose facilities and email types.
5. Select **Save**.

For a member, the facility choices are limited to that person's assigned facilities. An administrator can receive notifications for any active facility.

## Grievance subscriptions

Notification settings are separate from subscribing to an individual grievance. A subscription sends activity updates for that case, and Grievly may subscribe you automatically when you create or interact with it.

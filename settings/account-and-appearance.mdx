title: "Account and appearance"
description: "Manage personal account details, security, appearance, workspace selection, and sign out."
---

Your personal account controls are available from the avatar in the upper-right corner of CareResolve.

## Open account settings

1. Select your avatar.
2. Choose **Account settings**.
3. Use the account modal to move between your profile, security, notification, and appearance pages.

Profile and security are managed through CareResolve's authentication provider. Operational roles, facility access, organization provisioning, billing, and facility configuration remain CareResolve-managed workflows outside this personal account modal.

## Change appearance

Open **Account settings > Appearance**, then choose:

- **Light** for a bright daytime interface.
- **Dark** for reduced glare in low-light environments.

The appearance choice applies to CareResolve on the current device.

## Switch workspaces

If your account belongs to multiple organizations, select your avatar and choose **Switch workspace**. Select the organization you want to use from the workspace picker.

The switch action is hidden when your account belongs to only one organization. CareResolve also selects that organization automatically after sign-in. Contact your CareResolve account manager when another organization needs to be provisioned; the workspace picker does not create organizations.

If the selected workspace is still being activated, use the workspace switcher beside **Sign out** on the readiness screen.

## Sign out

Select your avatar and choose **Sign out**.

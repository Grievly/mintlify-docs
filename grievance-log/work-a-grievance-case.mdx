## Page anatomy

- **Header:** title, grievance ID, status, SLA context, and case actions.
- **Timeline:** intake record, AI summary, case notes, activity, attachments, and resolution actions.
- **Right rail:** subscription, assignee, due date, resident and reporter details, labels, patterns, attachments, and participants.

Use the three-dot header menu to print a summary or edit the title of an unlocked grievance. Administrators can delete an unresolved grievance; resolved grievances cannot be deleted.

## Work the case

1. Open **Intake record** to review the original submission.
2. Confirm the assignee, category, F-tag, and due context in the right rail.
3. Add case notes for investigation, follow-up, review, and coordination.
4. Upload supporting files from the Attachments panel.
5. Resolve the grievance, or submit it for approval when the facility requires sign-off.

Use `@` in a case note to mention a teammate and `#` to reference another grievance. Authors can edit or delete their own notes while the case is unlocked. The sparkle control can improve a draft note, but it never saves the note for you.

Keep PHI in the CareResolve case record and approved attachments rather than copying it into outside tools.

## Intake and guidance

An unlocked internal intake record can be corrected from its pencil action. Public submissions remain read-only so the submitter's original packet is preserved. Pending-approval and resolved records are also locked.

When an open grievance has an F-tag, the AI summary can show advisory **Suggested next steps** connected to federal Appendix PP guidance. Expanding the list or opening its source does not change the case or assign work.

## Right-rail controls

Subscribe when you need future activity notifications. Assignment, category, and F-tag can be updated while the grievance is open and not pending approval.

Attachments show scan state before Open or Download becomes available. Supported files may also include an AI summary; when the file may not match the concern, CareResolve keeps that summary collapsed behind a warning.

The Participants panel summarizes people who have touched the case. Pattern membership links open the related pattern without leaving the grievance.

## Locked case states

While resolution approval is pending, existing resolution fields, notes, attachments, and intake details are read-only. Users with case access may add an attachment, and administrators may append a case note. Cancel the approval request before changing existing case content.

After resolution, the existing case is read-only. Administrators may append a new note or attachment, and any user with case access can reopen the grievance when more work is required. Reopening preserves history and the original due date.

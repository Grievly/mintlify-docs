Use the grievance list to work open cases, find older records, and prepare for leadership or survey review.

Use the app-wide facility switcher first when you need to change facility scope. Then use the list filters to narrow the results.

## Open and resolved modes

**All grievances** shows open grievances by default.

Turn on **Show resolved grievances** to switch the list to resolved records. This is a separate resolved mode; it does not add resolved rows to the open list.

For top bar search, recently viewed grievances, result previews, and search-side filters, see [Navigate and search](/getting-started/navigate-and-search).

## Filter the list

Available filters in open mode include:

- **Date range** over the received date.
- **Status**: Pending Approval, Overdue, or High Priority. Open is the default list mode.
- **Category**.
- **F-tag**.
- **Assignee**, including Me or Unassigned.
- **Other**: Has attachments, Public submission, Submitted at Resident Council, Employee involved, Third party concern, or Anonymous reporter.

In resolved mode, the **Status** filter includes High Priority plus the advisory compliance-review results **Aligned**, **Minor gaps**, and **Significant gaps**. These review results appear only for resolved grievances with a current review for their selected F-tag.

Select **Reset filters** to clear page filters without changing a scoped queue such as My queue.

## Read list signals

The title column can show:

- A QR badge for a public submission.
- A paperclip and count when attachments are present.
- High Priority and Overdue markers.

In open mode, the **Due** column shows the SLA due state and any Pending Approval progress. In resolved mode, the **Resolved** column shows when the grievance was finalized and, when available, its advisory compliance-review result.

## Search the grievance list

Search can match case identifiers and narrative information across the grievance record, including titles, resident or involved-person information, case notes, attachment information, resolution fields, and timeline activity.

List search stays inside your facility access and the current open or resolved mode. For broader searching from anywhere in the app, use the top bar search.

## Sort the list

Open grievances default to **Created**, newest first. Resolved grievances default to **Resolved**, newest finalization first.

Select **ID**, **Received**, **Due** or **Resolved** as appropriate for the current mode, or **Created** to change the sort column or direction.

Sorting changes order only; it does not change which records match.

## Open or select grievances

Select a row to open the complete case record.

Use row checkboxes when you need a selected grievance export or printable PDF packet. Selected actions apply to the chosen records even when the table is paginated.

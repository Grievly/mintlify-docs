---
title: "Resolution approvals"
description: "Require selected people to approve a resolution before the grievance is completed."
---

Resolution approvals add facility-level sign-off before a grievance becomes resolved. The grievance remains open until every required signer approves or an administrator explicitly bypasses the remaining approvals.

## Configure required signers

Administrators configure approvals per facility:

1. Open **Facilities** and select the facility.
2. Open **Details** and find **Resolution approvals**.
3. Turn on **Require resolution approvals**.
4. Select one or more users who can access the facility.
5. Save.

CareResolve does not allow approvals without a signer. Organization administrators can be selected because they have organization-wide access.

## Submit for approval

When approvals are enabled, the case action says **Submit for approval** instead of **Resolve**.

Complete the resolution fields, submit, and confirm. CareResolve snapshots the required signer list at that moment; later facility-setting changes affect only future requests.

While approval is pending:

- The grievance remains unresolved and shows **Pending Approval**.
- Existing resolution fields, case notes, attachments, and intake details are read-only.
- Users with case access can add an attachment; administrators can append a note.
- Required signers are notified.
- An F-tagged Resolution shows an advisory compliance review above the approval actions.

The compliance review does not block approval. It is preserved when the unchanged Resolution becomes final.

## Approve the resolution

A required signer opens the grievance, reviews the resolution, selects **Approve**, completes the confirmation checklist, and confirms. The final required approval resolves the grievance.

If the submitter is also a signer, CareResolve records that person's approval at submission. When the submitter is the only signer, the action says **Resolve grievance** and resolves the case in the same confirmation flow.

## Administrator bypass

An administrator may select **Bypass remaining approvals** for an exception. Depending on whether the administrator is also an unsigned signer, the action becomes **Approve + bypass** or **Resolve**. The confirmation checklist still applies.

Use bypass intentionally: it completes the resolution without the outstanding signatures.

## Cancel, edit, resubmit, or reopen

The submitter, a configured signer, or an administrator can select **Cancel approval and edit**. Cancellation unlocks the open case and clears that request. A later submission creates a new signer snapshot, prepares a fresh compliance review when an F-tag is present, and requires every approval again.

Reopening a resolved grievance preserves its history and original due date but clears approval state from the active workflow. If approvals are still enabled, the new resolution must be submitted again under the current facility settings.

Required signers see cases awaiting their approval in **My queue**, even when they are not the assignee. The grievance log also provides a **Pending Approval** filter.

After finalization, an administrator or configured facility Resolution approver can correct a Resolution in place when its compliance review identifies gaps. **Update Resolution** preserves resolved status and completed approvals while recording the changes and rerunning the review.

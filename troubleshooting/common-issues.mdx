title: "Common issues"
description: "Troubleshoot common CareResolve access, grievance, report, and intake problems."
---

## My workspace is not ready yet

CareResolve is still preparing the selected organization. Account activation, billing, and facility provisioning are managed outside the product app.

Contact your CareResolve account manager if setup takes longer than expected. If your account belongs to multiple organizations, use the workspace switcher beside **Sign out** to open another workspace.

## I cannot see a facility

Ask an administrator to confirm your facility access in team settings.

If you are an admin, confirm the facility is active and that you are in the correct organization. Contact your CareResolve account team if the facility has not yet been provisioned or activated.

## I cannot find a grievance

Check filters, date range, status, and facility selection. Resolved records may be hidden depending on your current filters.

Also check whether you are in **All grievances**, **My queue**, **Overdue**, or **High Priority**. Those sidebar items apply different filters.

If you are using top bar search, confirm the facility switcher is set to the right scope and clear search-side filters such as received date, assignee, status, category, F-tag, and **Other**.

## I cannot find or link a meeting

Open **Meetings**, confirm the facility selector, and use meeting search to match the title or notes. Members only see meetings in assigned facilities.

A Resident Council grievance can link only to a meeting from the same facility. Confirm the grievance facility first, then retry **Link meeting** from the new-grievance composer or unlocked internal intake record.

## A meeting will not save or delete

A locked meeting is read-only until an administrator unlocks it. If another person edited the meeting, use **View latest** or refresh from the activity control before editing again.

Only administrators can delete meetings. A meeting with source-linked grievances cannot be deleted; remove or change each link from the corresponding grievance intake record first.

## I cannot resolve a grievance

Confirm that you can access the grievance and that it has enough follow-up to support the resolution.

Also confirm the grievance is open and not locked while waiting for approval. A required signer can approve a pending resolution; an administrator can cancel the request to unlock the case.

## I cannot update a finalized Resolution

**Update Resolution** appears when an F-tagged resolved grievance has compliance-review gaps and you are an administrator or a configured Resolution approver for that facility.

If approval is still pending, use **Cancel approval and edit** instead, then submit the revised Resolution for a fresh approval cycle.

## I cannot edit an F-tag

Confirm the grievance is still unlocked. Team members who can access an open grievance can review and edit its F-tags.

F-tags are read-only after the grievance is resolved or while a resolution is pending approval.

## Guidance or next steps are not showing

Confirm that the grievance has an F-tag and is in the right state. Suggested next steps appear only on open, unlocked grievances. Compliance review appears only after an F-tagged grievance is resolved.

Guidance-based items may not appear if the selected F-tag does not yet have a regulatory profile or if generation is still in progress. Open the case again later; a failed automatic generation is retried on a later visit.

## A High Priority grievance disappeared from the High Priority queue

Resolved grievances no longer appear in the High Priority queue.

Open the grievance from All grievances or adjust filters if you need to review the resolved record.

## My report is still generating

Large reports and reports with attachments can take longer. Refresh the Reports page or wait for the ready notification.

If a report fails, create it again with a smaller date range or without attachments.

The notification panel shows recent notifications from the last 30 days. If the report is older, open **Reports** directly.

## A public intake link is not working

The public intake URL is permanent, but it only accepts submissions while the facility is active.

Contact your CareResolve account team if the facility needs to be activated.

## I entered a complaint in the wrong facility

For an internal grievance, open the case, select **Intake record**, and use the pencil icon while the case is unlocked.

Public intake records are immutable. Add a case note that explains the correction and contact an administrator before creating a replacement record.

## My team member cannot be assigned to a department

Confirm the person has access to that facility.

Members must have facility access before they can be assigned to a department at that facility. Admins have access to all facilities.

## Assistant cannot answer about a grievance

Assistant respects role and facility access.

Confirm the grievance is in a facility the user can access and that filters or wording are not too narrow.

## I need support

Use the top bar help menu to contact support, give feedback, report a bug, or open these docs. You can also contact support at [support@careresolve.ai](mailto:support@careresolve.ai).

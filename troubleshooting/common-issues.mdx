---
title: "Common issues"
description: "Troubleshoot common Grievly access, grievance, report, and intake problems."
---

## I cannot see a facility

Ask an administrator to confirm your facility access in team settings.

If you are an admin, confirm the facility is active and that you are in the correct organization.

## I cannot find a grievance

Check filters, date range, status, and facility selection. Resolved records may be hidden depending on your current filters.

Also check whether you are in **All grievances**, **My queue**, **Overdue**, or **High Priority**. Those sidebar items apply different filters.

## I cannot resolve a grievance

Confirm the grievance has enough follow-up and that your role allows resolution actions.

Also confirm the grievance is still open. Resolved grievances are read-only in most areas.

## I cannot edit an F-tag

Only administrators can override F-tags.

Members can review suggested F-tags, but admin access is required to manually change them.

## A High Priority grievance disappeared from the High Priority queue

Resolved grievances no longer appear in the High Priority queue.

Open the grievance from All grievances or adjust filters if you need to review the resolved record.

## My report is still generating

Large reports and reports with attachments can take longer. Refresh the Reports page or wait for the ready notification.

If a report fails, create it again with a smaller date range or without attachments.

## A public intake link is not working

Confirm the facility link is current and that the facility is active.

Public intake is unavailable while a facility is archived.

## I entered a complaint in the wrong facility

Open the grievance and confirm whether your role allows editing facility assignment. If not, contact an administrator before duplicating the record.

Facility edits on grievance records may be restricted after resolution.

## My team member cannot be assigned to a department

Confirm the person has access to that facility.

Members must have facility access before they can be assigned to a department at that facility. Admins have access to all facilities.

## Assistant cannot answer about a grievance

Assistant respects role and facility access.

Confirm the grievance is in a facility the user can access and that filters or wording are not too narrow.

## I need support

Use **Get help** in the Grievly dashboard, open these docs, or contact support at [support@grievly.ai](mailto:support@grievly.ai).

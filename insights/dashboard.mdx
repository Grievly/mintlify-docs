---
title: "Dashboard"
description: "Review current workload, recent activity, and 30-day operating metrics."
---

The dashboard is the first place to review work that needs attention and recent grievance activity.

Use the facility switcher in the app navigation to change the dashboard scope. Administrators can choose any active facility; members can choose from facilities they can access.

## Administrator dashboard

The administrator view provides an organization or facility operating snapshot.

It can include:

- **Open grievances**.
- **Pending approval** resolutions.
- **Unassigned** open grievances.
- **Overdue** grievances and the share of currently open cases that are past due.
- Daily grievance volume over the last 30 days.
- High-priority intake count, share, and 30-day trend.
- New intake, resolved count, on-time resolution rate, and average resolution time for the last 30 days.
- Recent notification activity, including assignments, mentions, approvals, routing, and other relevant grievance events.
- Team load, including open and overdue assigned work by owner.

Select a snapshot or workload row to open the related grievance list. Select the 30-day activity card to continue into Analytics.

## Member dashboard

The member view focuses on personal work inside the selected facility scope.

It can include:

- Grievances resolved by you in the last 30 days.
- Your average resolution time for the last 30 days.
- Resolutions pending your approval, when applicable.
- Open grievances assigned to you.
- Recent notification activity for grievance events relevant to you.

Select a card or row to open the underlying cases.

## Interpreting the snapshot

Dashboard counts are shortcuts into current work. An overdue or High Priority marker is an operational signal, not a legal finding or a conclusion about the case.

The grievance record remains the source of truth for investigation, follow-up, and resolution.

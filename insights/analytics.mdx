---
title: "Analytics"
description: "Use analytics to review grievance volume, mix, timeliness, and trends."
---

Analytics helps teams review grievance trends over time.

Use it for leadership review, QAPI preparation, operational follow-up, and survey readiness.

## Filters

Analytics can be filtered by:

- Date range.
- Facility.

Facility filters respect your role and facility access.

## Widgets

Analytics can include:

- Volume trend.
- Top patterns.
- Time-to-resolution histogram.
- Source mix.
- Category mix.
- Status distribution.
- F-tag heatmap.
- Facility volume.
- Overdue by facility.
- Top employees mentioned.

Some widgets only appear when enough facilities are in scope. For example, facility comparison widgets need at least two facilities.

## Admin and member differences

Admins can see organization-wide analytics and admin-only views such as top employees mentioned.

Members see analytics scoped to their assigned facilities.

## Click-through review

Some widgets link back to filtered grievance lists.

Use the linked grievance list to review the actual cases behind the metric.

## Interpret carefully

Analytics shows trends and counts. It does not determine whether a grievance is substantiated, whether a regulatory event occurred, or whether a facility action was sufficient.

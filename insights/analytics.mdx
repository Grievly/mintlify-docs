---
title: "Analytics"
description: "Review grievance volume, mix, timeliness, and trends across an accessible date and facility scope."
---

Analytics helps teams review grievance trends for leadership, QAPI, operational follow-up, and survey preparation.

## Filters

Analytics uses a rolling 30-day window by default. Change the date range or select one or more facilities from the page filters.

Facility choices always respect your role and access.

## What analytics can show

| Widget | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| Grievance volume | Intake over the selected period |
| F-tag distribution | F-tag mix when exactly one facility is in scope |
| F-tag heatmap | Facility-by-F-tag comparison when at least two facilities are in scope |
| Location hotspots | Top unit or hall labels when exactly one facility is in scope, including Other and unknown groupings |
| Time to resolution | Days from received to resolved, including a 30-plus-day bucket and average |
| Category mix | Distribution by operational category |
| Status | Open and resolved mix |
| Source | Internal and public-intake mix |
| Public form submitters | Family member, resident, employee, and anonymous mix within public submissions |
| High-priority intake | Selected period versus the immediately preceding period of the same length |
| Facility volume | Volume comparison when at least two facilities are in scope |
| Top employees mentioned | Distinct grievances by involved employee, for administrators only |

Some facility-comparison widgets appear only when at least two facilities are in scope. **Location hotspots** appears only for a single-facility scope so unit and hall labels are not mixed across buildings.

Overdue work is intentionally shown on the Dashboard, Overdue queue, and grievance list rather than as a historical analytics widget. Overdue is a current case state, while Analytics date filters describe when grievances entered the selected reporting window.

## Compare High Priority intake

The High-priority widget compares both count and share with the prior period. For example, a 30-day selection is compared with the preceding 30 days.

Use both values together. A higher count can reflect higher overall intake even when the share is stable.

## Drill into the cases

Where a widget offers a link or selectable cell, open it to review the filtered grievance list behind the metric. The cases provide the context that a chart cannot.

## Admin and member differences

Administrators can review all accessible organization data and see **Top employees mentioned**. Members see analytics only for assigned facilities.

The employee widget counts distinct grievances that list each involved employee. It is an occurrence-frequency view, not a workload measure or finding of fault.

## Interpret carefully

Analytics shows trends and counts. It does not determine whether a grievance is substantiated, whether a regulatory event occurred, or whether a facility action was sufficient.

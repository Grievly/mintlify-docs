Assistant helps answer questions about grievance data you can access.

It can search, summarize, and cite relevant grievances, answer federal Appendix PP questions, explain how to use Grievly, and help you find public intake sharing tools. It does not replace the grievance record or human review.

## What Assistant can help with

Use Assistant for questions such as:

- Which grievances are overdue?
- What open concerns mention dietary service?
- Which High Priority cases need review?
- What patterns are active for a facility?
- Summarize recent grievances for leadership preparation.
- What does F585 require for a written grievance decision?
- Which Appendix PP guidance covers bed rails used as a restraint?
- How do I submit a resolution for approval?
- Give me the public intake link or QR code for a facility.

## Access scope

Assistant respects your role and facility access.

Admins can ask across the organization. Members receive answers scoped to facilities they can access.

If a request could apply to more than one facility, Assistant may ask you to choose one. If a user cannot get an answer about a grievance or facility, check their facility access.

When you ask for public intake sharing tools, Assistant can return the facility's permanent public intake URL and a QR card with **Copy link**, **Download poster**, and **Download PNG** actions.

## Federal regulatory questions

Assistant can retrieve federal Appendix PP guidance in two ways:

- For a specific F-tag or topic, it can open the corresponding regulatory profile.
- For a broader question, everyday wording, or an issue that spans several tags, it can search the stored Appendix PP passages and return the most relevant F-tags.

F-tag citations appear as selectable chips. Select a chip to open the tag's **Guidance**, **How to prepare**, and **Source** tabs.

Assistant's regulatory answers cover the federal corpus available in Grievly. They do not include state-specific requirements and should not be treated as legal advice or a compliance decision.

## Saved chats

Assistant chats are saved so you can return to prior conversations.

You can start a new chat, open an existing chat, or delete a chat when it is no longer useful.

## Citations, F-tags, and grievance cards

Assistant may include grievance cards, links to case records, or F-tag chips.

Open the grievance or guidance source to review the underlying material before relying on the answer.

## Use carefully

Assistant may summarize sensitive grievance information. Do not paste PHI into outside tools or use Assistant output as the complete case record.

AI output is advisory. Staff remain responsible for review, follow-up, resolution, and compliance decisions.

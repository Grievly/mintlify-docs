Grievly keeps daily work in two places: the top bar for fast actions and the sidebar for primary navigation.

## App layout

The top bar stays visible as you move through the app.

Use it to:

- Search grievances.
- Select **Create** to open the new grievance composer.
- Switch facility scope.
- Open notifications.
- Contact support, give feedback, report a bug, or open docs from the help menu.
- Open settings, account, theme, and sign-out actions from the menu on smaller screens.

The sidebar contains the main navigation and queue shortcuts, including Dashboard, grievance queues, Reports, Assistant, Insights, Team, Facilities, and Settings depending on your role.

On smaller screens, use the navigation button in the top bar to open the sidebar.

## Facility scope

Use the facility switcher in the top bar to choose the facility context for your work.

The selected facility scope affects dashboard metrics, grievance lists, search defaults, reports, and setup screens.

Admins can work across the organization or narrow to a facility. Members only see facilities they can access.

If a grievance is missing from search or a list, confirm the current facility scope before changing filters.

## Search grievances

Open search from the top bar. On smaller screens, use the search icon.

When search opens without a query, Grievly shows **Recently viewed** grievances. After you type, results switch to **Top matches**.

Search results can show:

- Status icons.
- Public-intake, attachment, and High Priority indicators.
- Grievance ID and title.
- Facility, assignee, created date, and summary preview on wider screens.

Select a result to open the grievance.

## Search filters

On wider screens, search includes a filter sidebar.

Use it to narrow results by:

- **Received**: Any time, Past 7 days, or Past 30 days.
- **Facility**.
- **Assignee**.
- **Status**: Open, Pending Approval, Overdue, or Resolved.
- **Category**.
- **F-tag**.
- **Other**: Has attachments, Public submission, Submitted at Resident Council, Employee involved, Third party concern, or Anonymous reporter.

Search only returns grievances inside your role and facility access.

## Notifications

Use the notification bell in the top bar to review recent activity.

The badge shows the unread count and caps large counts as **99+**.

The notification panel includes:

- An **Unread only** toggle.
- Actions for marking notifications read or unread.
- A panel action menu for bulk actions such as marking all notifications read.
- Notifications from the last 30 days.

Selecting a notification opens the relevant grievance, report, or app destination when one is available.

## Help and feedback

Use the help menu to contact support, give product feedback, report a bug, or open docs.

Feedback and support messages include your current app path so the Grievly team can understand where you were working.

Do not include resident names, room numbers, narrative PHI, attachments, or other sensitive case details in support or feedback messages unless your organization has approved that sharing path.

## Move around

Use the sidebar for the Dashboard, grievance queues, Meetings, Insights, Reports, Team, and Facilities. What appears depends on your role.

Use the top bar to:

- Search grievances.
- Open the **New grievance** composer from **Create**.
- Change facility scope.
- Review notifications or open help.
- Manage your account from your avatar.

On smaller screens, the navigation and search controls collapse into icons.

## Change facility scope

The facility switcher changes the scope for dashboard metrics, grievance lists, search defaults, reports, and setup screens.

Administrators can work across the organization or select one facility. Members see only facilities they can access. If a record seems missing, check facility scope before changing filters.

## Search grievances

Open the top-bar search or press **Command+K** on macOS or **Ctrl+K** on Windows.

- With no query, search shows recently viewed grievances.
- As you type, it shows the best matches inside your access scope.
- Results can include status, source and attachment indicators, grievance ID, title, facility, assignee, created date, and a short preview.

On wider screens, filter results by received date, facility, assignee, status, category, F-tag, attachments, intake source, Resident Council submission, employee involvement, third-party involvement, or anonymous reporter.

## Search meetings

Open **Meetings** and select its search action to search meeting titles and notes. Use the meeting-page facility selector to narrow the results, then open a result on its full page.

Meeting search is separate from the top-bar grievance search.

## Account, notifications, and help

Select your avatar to open **Account settings**, switch workspaces when you belong to more than one organization, or sign out. Account settings contains Profile, Security, Notifications, and Appearance.

The notification bell shows recent activity, unread state, and links back to the related record. Use its action menu to mark all notifications read or open notification preferences.

The help menu links to support, feedback, bug reporting, and these docs. Do not include resident names, room numbers, narratives, attachments, or other PHI in a support message unless your organization has approved that sharing path.

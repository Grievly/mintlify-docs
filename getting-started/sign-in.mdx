CareResolve uses secure sign-in tied to your organization. Your account controls which organization, facilities, and grievance records you can access.

## Sign in

1. Go to [app.careresolve.ai](https://app.careresolve.ai).
2. Sign in with your account.
3. If you belong to more than one organization, choose the organization you want to work in. CareResolve selects it automatically when you have access to only one.

## Choose the right workspace

If you support more than one organization or management group, confirm the active organization before creating or editing grievances. This keeps facility records, reports, and team access scoped to the correct account.

While using CareResolve, select your avatar in the upper-right corner and choose **Switch workspace** to change organizations. This action appears only when your account belongs to multiple organizations.

If the selected workspace is still being activated, the organization switcher remains available beside **Sign out**. Use it to move to another organization without refreshing the page manually.

Organizations are provisioned by CareResolve rather than created from the product app. Contact your CareResolve account manager when another organization needs to be added.

## If you cannot access the app

Contact your organization administrator and confirm:

- Your invitation was sent to the correct email address.
- You accepted the invitation.
- You were added to the correct organization.
- Your role has access to the facility or facilities you need.

---
title: "Workspace activation"
description: "Know what to expect while CareResolve prepares a new workspace for daily use."
---

CareResolve prepares new organizations, billing access, and the first facility with your account team. These activation tasks are not completed from the product app.

## While the workspace is being prepared

After signing in, you may see **Workspace is not ready yet.** This single readiness screen appears while CareResolve finishes any required account setup.

There is no retry button or separate billing or facility-provisioning step in the app. Contact your CareResolve account manager if activation takes longer than expected.

If you belong to more than one organization, use the workspace switcher beside **Sign out** to move to another organization while the selected workspace is still being prepared.

## After activation

Once the workspace is ready, administrators usually continue with:

- Team invitations and facility access.
- Facility locations for units, rooms, and common areas.
- Facility departments for routing.
- Public intake links and QR posters.
- Resolution approval signers.
- The facility's default grievance SLA.

CareResolve continues to manage organization creation, billing activation, and facility provisioning outside the product app. Contact your account manager when you need another organization or facility added or an account-level record changed.

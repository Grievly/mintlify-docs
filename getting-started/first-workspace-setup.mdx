---
title: "First workspace setup"
description: "Complete the setup gates required before a new Grievly workspace is ready for daily use."
---

New workspaces pass three readiness gates before teams can process live grievance information: an active Business Associate Agreement, billing activation, and at least one active facility.

An administrator signs the agreement. Grievly handles billing activation and facility provisioning with your account team.

## Setup order

1. Sign in and select the organization.
2. Have an administrator complete the Business Associate Agreement.
3. Coordinate billing activation with your Grievly account manager.
4. Wait for Grievly to provision the first active facility.
5. Invite team members.
6. Configure locations, departments, resolution approvals, and the facility SLA, then share the permanent public intake link or QR assets as needed.

## Business Associate Agreement

The Business Associate Agreement must be active before the workspace processes resident PHI.

Members cannot complete this step. They will see a waiting screen until an administrator completes it.

## Billing activation

After the agreement is active, Grievly activates billing for the workspace. There is no self-service Billing page in the product.

If activation is not ready, users see **Complete billing to continue**. Contact your Grievly account manager if the workspace should already be active.

## First facility

At least one active facility is required before the workspace can be used for daily grievance work.

Grievly provisions facilities for the organization. When the setup screen says the workspace is being prepared, use **Check again** after your account manager confirms provisioning.

## After setup

After the workspace is ready, administrators usually continue with:

- Facility locations for units, rooms, and common areas.
- Facility departments for routing.
- Public intake links and QR posters.
- Team invitations and facility access.
- Resolution approval signers.
- The facility's default grievance SLA.

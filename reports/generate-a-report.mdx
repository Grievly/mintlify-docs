---
title: "Generate a grievance log report"
description: "Create CSV, XLSX, or PDF reports from grievance data."
---

Reports help teams export grievance, complaint, and concern data for leadership review, QAPI, operational follow-up, and survey preparation.

Reports respect your role and facility access.

## Create a report

1. Open **Reports**.
2. Select **New report**.
3. Choose the grievance log report.
4. Select the format: CSV, XLSX, or PDF.
5. Choose filters such as facility, status, category, assignee, or date range.
6. Start the report.

## Generate from the grievance list

You can also generate a report from the grievance list.

Two modes are available:

- **Current view**: uses the current filters and includes all matching rows, not just the visible page.
- **Selected rows**: includes only the selected grievances.

Use selected rows when you need a targeted packet for a specific review.

## Download a report

Reports may take a moment to generate. When the report is ready, select **Download**.

The reports table shows whether a report is generating, ready, or failed.

## Include attachments

If available, choose the option to include attachments when you need the supporting case files in the export package.

When attachments are included, the download may be packaged as a ZIP file.

Large attachment reports can take longer.

## Report filters

Grievance log reports can use filters such as:

- Facility.
- Date range.
- Status, including Open, Resolved, Overdue, and High Priority.
- Category.
- F-tag.
- Assignee.
- Other filters such as Resident Council, employee involved, third party concern, or anonymous reporter.
- Search text from the current grievance list.

## Before sharing a report

Confirm the facility, date range, status filters, and included attachments. Reports may contain sensitive resident or reporter information, so share them only with approved team members.

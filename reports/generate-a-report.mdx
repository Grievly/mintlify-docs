---
title: "Generate a grievance log report"
description: "Create CSV, Excel, or PDF exports and printable case reports."
---

Reports help teams export grievance data for leadership review, QAPI, operational follow-up, and survey preparation. Every report respects your role and facility access.

## Create a filtered report

1. Open **Reports**.
2. Select **New report**.
3. Choose CSV, Excel, or PDF.
4. Set the facility, date, status, category, F-tag, assignee, or other filters you need.
5. Select **Generate Report**.

The Reports table shows when a file is generating, ready, or failed.

## Export selected grievances

From **All grievances**:

1. Select one or more rows.
2. Select **Export**.
3. Choose CSV, Excel, or PDF.
4. Generate the report.

Only the selected grievances are included. Their current list order is preserved.

## Print a summary or full report

After selecting grievance rows, choose **Print summary** instead of Export. You can generate:

- **Summary**: a concise printable case packet.
- **Full Report (internal use only)**: a richer internal packet that includes additional case metadata and activity history.

The action menu on an individual grievance also includes **Print summary**.

Both print modes use PDF. Review the output before sharing it outside the facility's approved workflow.

Open the notification bell to review recent report notifications. The notification panel keeps recent items for 30 days and can be filtered to unread notifications.

## Include attachments

The print dialog can include attachment files with the PDF. When selected, the download is a ZIP package containing the report, eligible clean attachments, and an attachment manifest.

Flagged, failed, or unavailable files are not included as downloadable attachment files. Large packages can take longer to generate.

## Report filters

Filtered grievance log reports can use:

- Facility and received-date range.
- Status, including Pending Approval, Overdue, and High Priority.
- Category and F-tag.
- Assignee.
- Other filters such as Has attachments, Public submission, Resident Council, employee involved, third party concern, or anonymous reporter.

## Download a report

When a report is ready, use its download action in the Reports table. A ready notification may also provide a direct **Download** action.

If a report fails, retry it or reduce the scope. Reports with many cases or attachments may take longer.

## Before sharing

Confirm the facility, date range, filters, print mode, and included attachments. Reports can contain sensitive resident, reporter, and case information, so share them only with approved people.
